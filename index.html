Our story

Our story began a decade ago, when our family decided to turn our love of flowers into a business. We started out with just a small storefront in Nis, but over the years we have grown to become one of the city's most trusted and beloved florists.
We take pride in being a part of the community and contributing to its beauty.
At Floweshop, we believe that every occasion deserves flowers. Whether you're celebrating a special milestone, expressing your love to someone special, or simply brightening up your own day, our skilled florists are here to create arrangements that are as unique and special as you are.
We also offer a variety of gifts, including candy box, chocolates, champagne, and more, to help you make any occasion even more special.

Thank you for choosing Floweshop for all your floral needs.
